üí∞ Revenue Generation Strategies

Pricing Psychology

  • Value-based pricing: Price based on customer outcomes
  • Anchor pricing: Use high-priced options to make others seem reasonable
  • Bundle strategies: Combine products for higher average order value
  • Tiered pricing: Offer basic, premium, and ultimate versions

Multiple Revenue Streams

  • One-time products: Guides, templates, and courses
  • Subscription models: Monthly resources and communities
  • Coaching/consulting: High-value personal services
  • Affiliate marketing: Promote complementary products
  • Licensing: Allow others to use your content

Conversion Optimization

  • A/B test everything: Headlines, prices, and call-to-actions
  • Social proof: Testimonials, case studies, and user counts
  • Urgency and scarcity: Limited-time offers and bonuses
  • Money-back guarantees: Reduce purchase risk
